# COURSE_001 INTAKE

## Purpose
This course is the foundational entry in the University. It establishes the core philosophy — **AI Fluency as human-AI collaboration** — and introduces the 4D Framework that will be the lens for all future learning.

## Why This Course
1. **Application relevance**: Directly supports Claude Corps application by demonstrating structured AI fluency thinking
2. **Framework value**: The 4D Framework (Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence) is a reusable mental model for all AI work
3. **Philosophical alignment**: The course's emphasis on "thinking with AI, not just about AI" matches the University's purpose

## Course Goals (from overview)
By the end, I will have:
- A framework to guide AI interactions
- Confidence in deciding when and how to engage AI effectively
- Practical skills for thoughtful human-AI collaboration
- Ability to evaluate and take responsibility for AI collaboration outcomes

## Key Concepts to Track
- AI Fluency: effective, efficient, ethical, safe collaboration
- The gap between "what's possible" and "what feels comfortable"
- Three interaction modes: Automation, Augmentation, Agency
- The 4Ds: Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence
- Core competencies vs. tactical skills (prompts that become outdated)

## Application Connection
This course provides:
- **Evidence of structured learning** from Anthropic's own educational content
- **Framework vocabulary** (4Ds) to discuss AI fluency knowledgeably
- **Philosophical grounding** for AI collaboration that goes beyond "prompt engineering"
- **Practical skills** demonstrated through exercises with Claude
